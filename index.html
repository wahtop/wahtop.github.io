History

Before the Pevensies came to power, Narnia was a dark, empty void. Aslan created Narnia through his breath and his voice and created all the creatures in the land. The White Witch, also known as Jadis who was from another land, ate a forbidden fruit which gave her immortality and got banished for 900 years. After 900 years, Jadis came back to subject Narnia to be a Totalitarian Government ruled by her. Under Jadis' Rule, Narnia changed to accomodate:

  1. White Witch's Secret Police
  2. No more Christmas
  3. No courts

This Government lasted 100 years and ended in the Winter Revolution when the Pevensie family and Aslan overthrew and killed her. Since then, Narnia has been ruled by the Pevensie family.
